# 批发商城管理端联调字段映射说明

## 1. 目标

本文档用于管理端前后端联调时统一字段口径，避免页面先按旧字段接入后再返工。

## 2. 平台工作台

- 接口：`GET /manager/dashboard/wholesale`
- 返回：`WholesaleDashboardVO`
- 关键字段：
  - `indexStatistics`
  - `storeRankList`
  - `goodsRankList`
  - `categoryRankList`
  - `profitSharingBalance`
  - `agentFundSummary`
  - `procurementSummary`
  - `storeAuditSummary`
  - `businessTrend`
  - `verificationSummary`
  - `governanceSummary`
  - `agentOverview`

## 3. 店铺治理

- 接口：`GET /manager/store/store/summary`
- 返回：`StoreAuditSummaryVO`
- 关键字段：
  - `totalCount`
  - `submittedCount`
  - `approvedCount`
  - `rejectedCount`
  - `frozenCount`
  - `openCount`
  - `closedCount`

- 接口：`GET /manager/store/store/audit/log/{storeId}`
- 返回：`List<StoreAuditLogVO>`

## 4. 订单治理

- 接口：`GET /manager/order/order/summary`
- 返回：`OrderManageSummaryVO`
- 关键字段：
  - `totalCount`
  - `totalFlowPrice`
  - `waitPayNum`
  - `waitShipNum`
  - `deliveredNum`
  - `waitCheckNum`
  - `waitSelfPickNum`
  - `finishNum`
  - `closeNum`

## 5. 售后治理

- 接口：`GET /manager/order/afterSale/summary`
- 返回：`AfterSaleManageSummaryVO`
- 关键字段：
  - `totalCount`
  - `applyCount`
  - `passCount`
  - `refuseCount`
  - `buyerReturnCount`
  - `waitRefundCount`
  - `completeCount`

## 6. 核销治理

- 接口：`GET /manager/other/verificationRecord/summary`
- 返回：`VerificationRecordSummaryVO`
- 关键字段：
  - `totalCount`
  - `successCount`
  - `failCount`
  - `exceptionCount`
  - `buyerSourceCount`
  - `storeSourceCount`
  - `managerSourceCount`

- 接口：`GET /manager/other/verificationRecord/exception/summary`
- 返回：`VerificationExceptionSummaryVO`
- 关键字段：
  - `exceptionCount`
  - `storeExceptionCount`
  - `managerExceptionCount`
  - `buyerExceptionCount`

## 7. 分账治理

- 接口：`GET /manager/profitsharing/summary`
- 返回：`ProfitSharingSummaryVO`
- 关键字段：
  - `totalCount`
  - `pendingCount`
  - `checkedCount`
  - `completedCount`
  - `totalBillAmount`
  - `totalCommissionAmount`
  - `totalRefundAmount`

## 8. 代理商对账

- 接口：`GET /manager/agent/reconciliation/overview`
- 返回：`AgentReconciliationOverviewVO`
- 关键字段：
  - `agentCount`
  - `procurementSummary`
  - `fundSummary`

## 9. 联调约定

- 列表页统一使用分页参数，不在前端自造分页语义。
- 状态文案统一由后端枚举映射，不在前端重新命名。
- 审核、冻结、解绑、退款、核销等动作，必须先查详情再做操作。
- 页面如需补字段，优先以本说明中的返回 VO 为准补齐。
